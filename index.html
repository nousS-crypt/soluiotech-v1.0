About SOLUIOTECH

SOLUIOTECH (Solution Using Internet of Things and Technology) is a community of robtics enthusiasts. Our mision at SOLUIOTECH is to create innovate solution using IoT to solve everyday challenges faced by individuals and industries.

Our start-up is filled with passionate experts eager to share their skills. Here, learning and innovation are at the heart of our community. Join us to grow in a collaborative environment where every talent is valued.

Latest Awards

We are thrilled to announce that our team secured first place in the Cars4Mars African Rover Challenge, competing against over 240 participants. Our success is a testament to our dedication, expertise, and collaborative spirit. We bring innovation to the forefront, proving that when passionate minds unite, the sky is not the limit—Mars is!
